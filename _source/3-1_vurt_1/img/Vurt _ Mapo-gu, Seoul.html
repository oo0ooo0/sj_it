INFO

vurt.
Techno Club located in Hapjeong, Seoul
Emerged in 2014 with the slogan of 'Ancient Future', vurt. is a group that represents timeless electronic music and the pure spirit of underground. By activating the venue which has the same name of itself, it has been organizing underground parties every month. Mainly with underground techno artists in Seoul, vurt. has been gathering international techno artists from Europe, North America and Japan and setting forth a new underground culture in Seoul. The venue opens 3-4 times in a month with occasional events.
